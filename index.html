Use the left and right arrow keys to avoid the incoming asteroids. Hitting an asteroid head on
or moving into the side of one will result in the crashing of your team's alien spaceship. You'll earn $0.001 per second
that you play the game. The game will end once you reach the maximum reward, $2.00, or you collide with
an asteroid.
This version of the game is a team one, meaning you'll play along anyone else playing with your current setting (mediator=average/better).
Your individual position is marked by the green square and your team's position is that of the alien spaceship.
When the team's alien spaceship collides with an asteroid, the game is over for everyone on the team.
Your green square is free to move about and get hit with asteroids without it counting. However, the position of your green square
influences the position of your team's spaceship. When "mediator=average" the spaceship's position is that of average of every player's
current position. When the "mediator=better" each player's position has a different weight on the collective spaceship's position
depending on how long they've survived in the game. Best of luck, your team is counting on you!

Note: Moving off the screen to the left will result in your ship appearing on the far right and vice-versa.
     Also, when you press the "Start Game" button, you'll get a 3 second countdown before the game begins, so get into position!
